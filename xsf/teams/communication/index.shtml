XSF Communication Team

The XSF's Communication Team is responsible for maintaining and improving the websites and other written materials produced by the XSF. Its particular areas of responsibility include:

  • The xmpp.org website, including content, navigation, and "look and feel"
  • Proofreading and copy editing of XSF specifications (XEPs)
  • The XSF weblog at blog.xmpp.org
  • The wiki at wiki.xmpp.org
  • Production of whitepapers
  • Conference materials, brochures, and other marketing materials
