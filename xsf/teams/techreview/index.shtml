XSF Technical Review Team

The XSF's Technical Review Team is responsible for completing technical reviews of XMPP Extension Protocols (XEPs). Although the team's reviews are purely advisory and do not have any official weight within the XSF's standards process (e.g., as formal input to voting by the XMPP Council), such reviews help the XSF improve the XMPP protocol stack. The team can review specifications on its own initiative or when requested to do so by the XMPP Council, and at any stage in the XSF's standards process, including:

  • Before a "ProtoXEP" is accepted for publication by the XMPP Council
  • While a XEP is in the Experimental state
  • During a Last Call related to advancement of a specification from Experimental to Draft (or Active)
  • While a XEP is in the Draft state
  • During a Call for Experience related to advancement of a specification from Draft to Final
  • While a XEP is in the Final state

Reviews generally follow the template and are sent to the standards@xmpp.org list.
